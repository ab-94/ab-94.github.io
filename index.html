Project Idea

Z-Tap

Overview

Z-Tap is a single-player android mobile game that sets the player in a post-apocalyptic world of zombies but mostly text-based, it will also have a random event generator each time the player makes a move! (this could trigger interaction with a zombie or human, it could mean finding a house to loot and over 20 other random events!). The player will have to travel, forage for food, find clean water, fight zombies, embark in 'delves' (delves are the equivalent of say an instance or 'Dungeon' in a game like World of Warcraft, Elder Scrolls Online etc.) the player will have talent trees, be able to craft over 50 items, a loot system that will have 50+ weapons, head to the marketplace to haggle prices risking reputation or getting rare items dirt cheap!

Motivation

It's no doubt that if you're a gamer from noob to hardcore you have undoubtely played at least one game with zombies in it. Zombie games are ever-growing and become more and more innovative as we go ahead in time. This makes most gamers explore many different types of zombie games. Z-Tap is a partial text-based game and i say partial because there will be animations, graphics depicting simple tasks (showing their character is moving but no background or landscape for example). As far as my research has gone there is no other game like this and to have it available in your pocket for when your on your work break, waiting for the kids to come out of school it seems like an attractive option.

Description

    - 50+ craftable items. (bandages, med-kits, splints, slings, sewing kits, ointment, rubbing alcohol, headwrap, shirts, pants, footwraps, helmets, boots, spears, knives, baseball bat, pick axes, bows etc.)
    - Thirst and starvation mechanics.
    - Interactable NPCs. (bandits, shopkeeps, non-threatening survivors etc.)
    - Talent tree system. (Two-handed, One-handed or dual-wield specialization. Bladed, Blunt or Ranged Specialization etc.)
    - Inventory system. (The player will be able to store and stack items and use them for crafting and or selling!)
    - Delves. (I coined this term instead of the over-used 'instance' or 'dungeon' that is used in most RPG games and MMORPGs, this will give the player a true challenge and test their abilities in combat against a variety of mini-bosses and bosses that will have specific mechanics in each delve. This will keep the player on their toes and to expect anything!)
    - Unique combat system. (Z-Taps combat system will give the opportunity and challenge for the player to 'dodge' enemy attacks, a button will pop up on the screen when the enemy is preparing to attack and the player will have a certain amount of time to react to that button depending on the difficulty chosen and type of enemy that they are facing. The same goes for player attacking, after dodging they will have a 'counter-attack' opportunity and this will deal more damage, if they miss the counter attack then the player will strike the enemy with normal damage.)
    - Random Event Generation (The random event generation feature will give the players the feeling that everything that happens in the world would be and IS random. With over 20+ events that could happen depending on the area that you are in this feature will create a real world illusion and really immerse the players into a truly terrifying world where anything can happen. More features are and will be added with DLC in the future)
    - The World (The actual world that the player will be adventuring through will be a randomly generated world, consisting of at least 6 cities, 24 suburbs, 24 towns, 30 small-towns & over 50 rural areas. This feature will be subject to balancing and or user-input but the template i have set will give the player plenty and plenty of content and interesting interactions.)
    - Loot and Marketplace (In the Z-Tap world when you kill any enemy they will have a chance at dropping an item and or 'Tokens' items can be sold or used in combat and tokens are used for purchasing items from the 'Z-Market' the Z-Market will only be available at certain parts of each area you are in and will only generate new items every 30 minutes in real-time. The player will also be able to buy other items such as food and water but these are the most expensive things to buy as it is dangerous to acquire them.)
    - Haggling (Haggling items happens in the 'Z-Market' which was just explained above. Haggling can be rewarding and detramental to your reputation so this feature must be used wisely by the player because if you annoy a vendor there is a high chance that the news will spread about you and prices for you could sky-rocket or the vendors will not sell to you at all until you perform enough good deeds that your reputation is restored.)
    - Reputation (Having a good reputation in the world makes all the difference with non-threatening survivors, vendors and also your general karma. Bad deeds do the same thing but have the opposite effect to performing good deeds. non-threatening survivors will quickly become violent with you, vendors may not sell to you or sell at ridiculous prices, and bandits will be after you head. Good deeds are harder and a more rewarding over time. Where as bad deeds are easy and reward you straight away with loot or tokens, but beware this paints a target on the players head and surviving will becoming much harder in the long run.)

Technology & Tools

The tech and tools required to develop this game would include desktop computers for the developers that are capable of running Unity Editor for programming and overall game layout, Photoshop for creating any sprites that would be needed and Audacity & a high quality microphone with some sound proofing equipment which would cover sound effect recording and editing. Additionally the developers would need a variety of android phones to test features and perform quality assurance testing on the project this could include different versions of Samsung Phones, Oppo, Google, Asus, Nokia etc. The reasoning behind having different brands and types is so that we can give as many users as we could the best possible experience with the game and ensure that majority of the bigger brands are covered.

Skills Required

Skills required would be an Audio Engineer to produce the sound effects for the game, programmers that are familiar with C# programming as that is what i am currently most experienced in myself, 2D artists that can create sprites and UI images for the game. Finding these skills could be in the moderate range i believe but it would be as simple as posting a job ad on Seek.com.au or by word of mouth. I believe that there are plenty of capable people out there that would be willing to work on this project.

Outcome

As ambitous as i am, i believe the outcome of a project such as this would be average to below average depending on how the game is marketed and so forth, but i also firmly believe that this project when finished would push more limits on the zombie genre which would lead to more innovative ideas and out of the box thinking when it comes to these type of games. In summary I wouldn't expect the project to do insanely well or even reach an average in terms of populatiry, but i believe it would be a great stepping stone especially for myself. In turn perhaps inspire others to push the boundaries of game development.
